The best websites for UX inspiration

Jonathan Hunter 14/02/2016

Finding Inspiration

Inspiration is everywhere from the moment you turn on your computer or look at your smartphone UX design is the first and foremost thing that you will experience. In fact, UX is a part of device manufacturing and so even the action of turning on your phone was pre thought out by a designer. Start to look around you differently and you will notice the intent of design all around you.

Becoming part of communities both on and offline can also help you get inspiration and see how other people experience various designs and user experiences. Remember not everyone will experience things in the same way. UI (user interface) and UX design is as much of an art form as it is a skill. I studied music and when learning to improvise in Jazz we had to learn a ton of little snippets so that when we took solo's we could have reference material at our fingertips. I find web design similar in some ways as there are many design elements that are crucial to a great looking web page and knowing how to implement each one quickly or even grabbing some code from a previous project will not only save you time when designing but will keep your designs solid and sexy.

![Site Inspire Homepage](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/JON4TH4NHUNT3R/worst-dev-ever/gh-pages/assets/images/blog/site-inspire.png "Site Inspire") [Site Inspire](https://www.siteinspire.com)

This showcase site is a pretty good resource but they only update the site every few days. On average they showcase 1 site per day though some days they will release 4 or 5 showcases and other days none. They find some very cool sites that implement some great techniques to keep a user engaged. You can submit your website design and they may feature it as well. They provide the link of each website so you can see it in action. They mostly showcase websites that are animated in some way.

![Mobile Patterns Homepage](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/JON4TH4NHUNT3R/worst-dev-ever/gh-pages/assets/images/blog/mobile-patterns.png "Mobile Patterns") [Mobile Patterns](https://www.mobile-patterns.com)

Mobile patterns is similar to Site Inspire in many ways except it features only mobile designs. 2015 was known as the year of "thinking mobile first" when it came to great UX and UI design and so this resource has exploded with great submisions of mobile designs. You can also view by various elements such as calendars, popovers, sign up flows, splash screens and more.

![Awwwards Homepage](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/JON4TH4NHUNT3R/worst-dev-ever/gh-pages/assets/images/blog/awwwards.png "Awwwards") [Awwwards](https://www.awwwards.com) Awards is also a showcase website with the added benefit of being Site Of the Day if your submitted site is the best design of the day. Finding inspiration and taking a look at website designs on Awwwards is a great way to see where design is moving in 2016 and keeping up with the latest techniques. I will update this list as I find more inspirational sites to get ideas for your next web project.
